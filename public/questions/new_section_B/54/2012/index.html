What a rainy season it was
The season of heavy rains.
That destroyed almost everything.
Without sparing people’s lives.

What a rainy season it was!
The season of heavy rains.
That hit the ground severely.
And hurt people’s crops.

What a rainy season it was!
The season of heavy rains.
That demolished people’s houses.
As well as livestock, trees and other property.

What a rainy season it was!
The season of heavy rains.
That caused floods and landslides.
Which left us homeless.

Natale Geraldine
