Read the poem below carefully and then answer in full sentences the questions that follow.

I connect everybody in the world
Ready to serve if you feed me
Airtime is my favorite meal
Measured according to the user’s pocket
From as low as five hundred shillings.

I am mobile and easy to carry,
In the pocket of hand bad’ I am present
Ready to be used
To send or receive a message
As the user may choose.

I connect people near and far
To talk and laugh
But for seconds, minutes or hours
But I quickly switch off
When my meal gets finished.

I can be your bank
As I keep or carry your money safely
Without using even a coin
I deliver it all to the receiver
To pay fees or solve other problems
