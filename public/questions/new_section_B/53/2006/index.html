Read the poem below and answer in full sentences the questions that follow.

Where is my nice pen?
I want to write letter
To my children
And send them money.

I will not tell them
I am here
I don’t want them
To know that I am
A prisoner,
I want them to grow up
Without suffering,
I want them to pass
Their examinations
And get good jobs
And buy land,
Houses,
Cars……

I do not want my children
To get shocked,
I do not want them
To feel sad and sorry
And cry for me,
I do not want them to know
That my hands and feet
Are tied with ropes
And I am sitting
On the naked thigh
Of the stone floor
……………………

(Okot p’Bitek: in the anthology: Attachments to the sun, Arnold.)
