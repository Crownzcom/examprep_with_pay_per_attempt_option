Read the poem below and answer in full sentences the questions that follow.

I love our village.
A village full of forgiveness;
We all live in peace;
The peace we protect and enjoy.

I like our village.
A village full of love for one another;
We protect our animals, gardens and homes;
Education, good health are ours.

I like our village.
A village of wise people;
Elders give words of wisdom to the young;
We, their dearest children, listen in humility.

Oh! Peaceful Malala Village!
Let other villages admire and learn from us;
To love our motherland, Uganda;
We are one people, together we stand strong.

Nyayuk pa Achieng
